About KATAMI

KATAMI brings you the authentic taste of Japanese confectionery, carefully crafted using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Our name "KATAMI" (形見) means "keepsake" or "memento" - representing our commitment to preserving the beautiful traditions of Japanese dessert making while sharing these treasures with the world.
